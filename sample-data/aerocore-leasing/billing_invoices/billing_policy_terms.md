# AeroCore HaaS Billing Terms and Policy

*   **Subscription Cycles:** Monthly leases are billed in advance on the 1st of each month. Payments are due within 15 days of invoice generation.
*   **Overage Billing:** Overage flight hours are calculated using recorded GPS flight logs. Hours are rounded up to the nearest quarter hour. Overage is billed in arrears on the 1st of the following month.
*   **Damage Billing:** Accidental damage outside warranty is billed at cost of replacement parts plus a technician labor rate of $120.00/hour.
