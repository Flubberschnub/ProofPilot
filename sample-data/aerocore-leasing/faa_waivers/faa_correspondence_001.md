# FAA Inquiry Correspondence: Weight Class Authorization
## Case Ref: FAA-INQ-990812-SM4

*   **To:** Carlos Mendez, Lead Field Engineer, AeroCore Leasing
*   **From:** FAA Aviation Safety Inspector, Des Moines FSDO
*   **Date:** 2026-02-18
*   **Subject:** Maximum Takeoff Weight (MTOW) of AeroCore SprayMaster v4

### Content
AeroCore's SprayMaster v4 has an empty weight of 24.5 kg, but carries a maximum liquid tank payload of 30 kg, bringing the MTOW to 54.5 kg (120.1 lbs). Drones over 55 lbs require a specific exemption under **Section 44807** rather than standard Part 107 regulations. Please confirm that operations of the SprayMaster v4 will keep tank capacity limited to 24.9 kg to remain under the 55 lbs Part 107 threshold, unless operating under AeroCore's Section 44807 exemption.
