# FAA Airspace Authorization: Altitude Waiver (High Mapping)
## Waiver Reference Number: FAA-ALT-88390

*   **Granted To:** AeroCore Leasing & Field Services, Inc.
*   **Effective Date:** 2025-08-01 to 2027-08-01
*   **Authorized Operations:** Operation of UAS above 400 feet AGL under Title 14 CFR § 107.51.

### Provisions & Limitations
1.  **Max Altitude:** Maximum allowed altitude is 750 feet AGL.
2.  **Safety Buffer:** Must operate at least 2 miles away from active airport approach pathways.
3.  **Transponder:** Drone must be equipped with active ADS-B Out hardware broadcasting coordinates.
