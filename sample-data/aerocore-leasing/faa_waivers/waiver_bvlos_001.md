# FAA Form 7711-1 Airspace Authorization (BVLOS Excerpt)
## Waiver Reference Number: FAA-BVLOS-88391

*   **Granted To:** AeroCore Leasing & Field Services, Inc.
*   **Effective Date:** 2025-10-15 to 2028-10-15
*   **Authorized Operations:** Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS) operations under Title 14 CFR § 107.31.
*   **Approved Aircraft:** AeroCore SprayMaster v4 (SM4), DJI Matrice 300 RTK.
*   **Operations Area:** Agricultural zones in Polk County, Iowa (Sector 4 coordinates: 41.683419 N, -93.629103 W).

### Provisions & Limitations
1.  **Visual Observers:** At least one trained Visual Observer (VO) must be positioned to monitor airspace integrity.
2.  **Telemetry Uplink:** Real-time cellular LTE telemetry backup must be active. Flight automatically aborts if ping exceeds 250ms.
3.  **Maximum Speed:** Ground speed must not exceed 30 knots during BVLOS execution.
