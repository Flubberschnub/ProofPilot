# FAA Airspace Authorization: Multiple UAS Operation (Swarming)
## Waiver Reference Number: FAA-SWARM-44021

*   **Granted To:** AeroCore Leasing & Field Services, Inc.
*   **Effective Date:** 2025-06-15 to 2026-06-15
*   **Authorized Operations:** Operation of multiple small unmanned aircraft systems simultaneously under Title 14 CFR § 107.35.

### Provisions & Limitations
1.  **Pilot Limit:** One pilot may operate up to 3 SprayMaster v4 drones concurrently.
2.  **Safety Lead:** A dedicated safety supervisor must monitor telemetry link status at all times.
3.  **Software Control:** Autonomous ground control software (AeroLink v4.1) must handle collision avoidance constraints.
