# FAA Airspace Authorization: Night Operations Waiver
## Waiver Reference Number: FAA-NIGHT-99281

*   **Granted To:** AeroCore Leasing & Field Services, Inc.
*   **Effective Date:** 2025-01-01 to 2027-12-31
*   **Authorized Operations:** Operations at night under Title 14 CFR § 107.29.
*   **Approved Aircraft:** AeroCore SprayMaster v4, DJI Matrice 300 RTK.

### Provisions & Limitations
1.  **Strobe Lighting:** UAS must be equipped with anti-collision strobe lighting visible for at least 3 statute miles.
2.  **Pilot Training:** Deployed pilots must complete AeroCore's night flying training curriculum.
3.  **Target Area Identification:** Landings zones must be illuminated with at least 5 foot-candles of light.
