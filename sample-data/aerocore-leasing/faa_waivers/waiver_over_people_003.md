# FAA Airspace Authorization: Operations Over People
## Waiver Reference Number: FAA-OOP-33921

*   **Granted To:** AeroCore Leasing & Field Services, Inc.
*   **Effective Date:** 2026-03-01 to 2027-02-28
*   **Authorized Operations:** Category 3 UAS operations over human beings under Title 14 CFR § 107.39.
*   **Approved Aircraft:** DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise (with AeroCore parachute integration).

### Provisions & Limitations
1.  **Parachute Recovery:** Autonomous parachute deployment system must be armed and functional.
2.  **Maximum Impact Energy:** Kinetic energy on impact must be validated below 25 ft-lbs.
3.  **Warning signs:** Warning signs must be posted around the active mapping grid boundaries.
