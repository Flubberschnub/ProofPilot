# Battery Safety & Handling Guidelines
## Model: AeroCore BATT-SM4-0029311

*   **Chemistry:** Lithium-Polymer (LiPo) 12S
*   **Capacity:** 22,000 mAh
*   **Optimal Charging Temperature:** 15°C to 40°C
*   **Critical Storage Warning:** Always store batteries at 50% charge (3.82V per cell). Do not leave fully charged batteries for more than 4 days.
*   **Degradation Limit:** Retire cells when capacity falls below 80% of nominal baseline (approx. 17,600 mAh).
