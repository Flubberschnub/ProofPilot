# Carbon Rotor Assembly & Mounting Specifications
## Part Reference: PART-PROP-SM4

*   **Diameter:** 28 inches
*   **Pitch:** 9.2 inches
*   **Material:** 3K Twill Carbon Fiber
*   **Mounting Torque:** Tighten rotor screws to exactly 4.2 Nm. Under-tightening causes high vibrations; over-tightening fractures the carbon arms.
