# AeroCore SprayMaster v4 — Technical Specifications
## Category: Heavy-Lift Agricultural UAS

*   **Manufacturer:** AeroCore Robotics LLC
*   **Model Number:** AC-SM4-H
*   **Empty Weight:** 24.5 kg
*   **Maximum Takeoff Weight (MTOW):** 54.5 kg (under Part 107 limit if tank payload is kept under 25 kg)
*   **Motor Type:** Brushless Outrunner Direct Drive (KV-120)
*   **Rotor Configuration:** Octocopter (8 carbon rotors)
*   **Tank Capacity:** 30 Liters (liquid chemical payload)
*   **Max Battery Flight Time:** 18 minutes (fully loaded), 35 minutes (empty tank)
*   **Communication Range:** 5.0 km (dual LTE cellular backup)
