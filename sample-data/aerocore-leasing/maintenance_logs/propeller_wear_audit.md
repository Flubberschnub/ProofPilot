# Structural Propeller Fatigue Audit Report
## Department: Engineering

*   **Author:** Carlos Mendez
*   **Findings:** Reviewed 12 instances of carbon propeller micro-cracks over the last 90 days. Over 80% of cracks occurred at the root mounting screws, likely due to technicians applying torque exceeding the 4.2 Nm specification. Ordered torque drivers pre-set to 4.2 Nm for all depots.
