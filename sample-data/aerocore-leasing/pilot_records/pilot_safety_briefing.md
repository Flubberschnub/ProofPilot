# Pilot Safety Briefing: Weather & Wind Protocols
## Document Code: PL-SAFE-002

*   **Standard Rule:** Drones must not take off if average wind speed exceeds 20 mph, or wind gusts exceed 25 mph.
*   **Rain Policy:** Do not operate SprayMaster v4 in heavy rain, as moisture can short-circuit the ESC motor controllers.
*   **Emergency Landing:** If structural warning or battery voltage error displays in the AeroLink app, the pilot must execute emergency landing in a cleared agricultural zone immediately.
