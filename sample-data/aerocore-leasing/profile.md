# AeroCore Leasing & Field Services — Company Profile

AeroCore Leasing & Field Services, Inc. (AeroCore) is a premier B2B industrial equipment leasing and operations management company. Founded in 2021, AeroCore specializes in leasing high-value autonomous hardware—specifically industrial-grade mapping drones, agricultural spraying drones, and multi-spectral environmental sensors—to enterprise clients in agriculture, construction, energy, and environmental surveying. 

AeroCore does not just lease equipment; it provides a comprehensive "Hardware-as-a-Service" (HaaS) model, which includes hardware provisioning, certified pilot deployment, preventative maintenance, real-time telemetry tracking, and regulatory compliance auditing.

## 🎯 Target Customer Profiles
AeroCore services mid-market and enterprise companies across three primary segments:
1.  **Industrial Agriculture:** Large-scale farms and cooperatives that lease autonomous spraying and crop-health monitoring drones.
2.  **Infrastructure & Construction:** Engineering firms that lease mapping drones to construct 3D CAD meshes of building projects.
3.  **Energy & Utilities:** Wind, solar, and powerline operators that require automated thermal inspections and LiDAR scanning.

## 🛠️ Organizational Departments
To run this complex operation, AeroCore is split into six distinct departments:
*   **Sales & Client Management:** Manages the pipeline of prospective clients, generates custom lease quotes, and drafts service agreements.
*   **Billing & Finance:** Handles recurring subscription invoicing, usage-based billing adjustments (flight hour tracking), parts procurement, and accounts receivable.
*   **Dispatch & Operations:** Coordinates certified drone pilots, schedules field technicians, optimizes travel routes, and tracks regulatory approvals (FAA Waivers).
*   **Warehouse & Logistics:** Manages the physical inventory of drones, spare parts, camera attachments, battery packs, and handles shipping/receiving.
*   **Field Engineering & Maintenance:** Conducts repairs, performs preventative maintenance calibrations, and inspects damaged hardware returned by clients.
*   **Technical Support & Customer Care:** Receives client issues, manages helpdesk tickets, monitors remote telemetry alerts, and coordinates hardware troubleshooting.

## 📜 Compliance & Safety Standards
Operating autonomous commercial hardware in national airspace requires strict adherence to federal and local regulations:
*   **FAA Part 107:** All pilots deployed by AeroCore must be certified Part 107 Remote Pilots.
*   **FAA Section 44807 / waivers:** AeroCore handles complex flight waivers (e.g., flying over people, night operations, beyond visual line of sight - BVLOS).
*   **SOC 2 Type II:** AeroCore audits its client portals and telemetry database to secure sensitive enterprise spatial and construction data.
*   **ISO 27001:** Information security standards governing AeroCore's proprietary mapping analysis servers.
