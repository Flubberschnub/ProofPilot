### ITEM 1. BUSINESS OVERVIEW

AeroCore Leasing & Field Services, Inc. ("AeroCore," "the Company," "we," "us," or "our") is a leading provider of enterprise Hardware-as-a-Service (HaaS) and operations management for autonomous aerial hardware and industrial multi-spectral sensors. We serve clients across agricultural crop-spraying, civil infrastructure monitoring, and utility inspections. 

Our business model relies on two primary revenue streams:
1.  **Fixed Lease Subscriptions:** Long-term leases (12 to 36 months) of our drone fleets, which include standard replacement batteries and camera attachments.
2.  **Usage and Service Billings:** Variable, usage-based fees calculated per flight-hour exceeded (above a standard 40-hour monthly allowance), plus ad-hoc technician dispatch fees for hardware calibrations and repairs.

#### Strategic Growth Initiatives & Corporate Goals
For FY2026, our key strategic initiatives include:
*   **Operational Automation:** We aim to reduce our administrative overhead by 20% by automating manual data reconciliation processes between our remote drone telemetry databases and our billing systems.
*   **API-First Ecosystem Integration:** To capture enterprise clients, we must integrate our flight logging systems directly with downstream client databases, such as CargoWise for logistics clients and Salesforce CRM for corporate accounts.
*   **Strict Regulatory Adherence:** Maintaining 100% compliance with FAA Part 107 regulations and expanding our automated LAANC (Low Altitude Authorization and Notification Capability) booking coverage to reduce wait times for mission dispatch from 24 hours to under 5 minutes.

# AeroCore Press Release (For Immediate Release)
## AeroCore Announces Landmark Multi-Year Fleet Lease with Apex Agriculture Solutions, LLC

**AUSTIN, TX — April 18, 2026** — AeroCore Leasing & Field Services, Inc. (EIN: 87-4491029) announced today that it has signed a multi-year fleet lease agreement (Contract Ref: LS-88392) with Apex Agriculture Solutions, LLC (DUNS: 081129843). Under the terms of the agreement, AeroCore will deploy a fleet of its new agricultural drones—specifically the AeroCore SprayMaster v4 (Serial: SM4-900821-X)—equipped with MultiSpectral-CropSensor Pro sensors to Apex's major farming cooperatives throughout Polk County, Iowa.

"This lease marks a significant milestone in agricultural automation," said Robert Chen, VP of Operations at Apex Agriculture. "By combining AeroCore's Hardware-as-a-Service lease structure with certified pilot dispatch, we expect to optimize our crop health monitoring and reduce manual chemical application tracking times significantly. However, a key success factor will be the integration of real-time flight telemetry directly into our logistics ledger."
